BucketHeap Interview Questions
==============================

ALGORITHMS & SEARCH
-------------------
1. What is the difference between A*, Weighted A*, Anytime A*, and ANA*? When would you choose each?

2. ANA* calls rebuild() every time it finds a new incumbent solution. Why? What breaks if you skip it?

3. What is the priority function E(n) = (G_upper - g(n)) / h(n) in ANA*, and what does maximizing it mean intuitively?

4. Dynamic Potential Search uses a focal list. How does it differ from ANA* in how it balances optimality vs. speed?

5. What does "bounded suboptimal" mean? If alpha=1.5, what guarantee does the solution carry?

6. How does the stale-pop strategy work, and why use it instead of decrease-key?

DATA STRUCTURES & DESIGN
------------------------
7. Walk me through the two-level bucket queue. What does the primary bucket index represent? The secondary?

8. Why does BucketHeap combine a TwoLevelBucketQueue with an IndexedDAryHeap rather than using either alone?

9. What are the alpha and beta parameters in BucketHeap? How do they aggregate bucket indices?

10. Why store node IDs as uint32_t indices into a pool rather than raw pointers? What are the tradeoffs?

11. BlockPool allocates fixed 512-byte blocks holding 126 node IDs each. Why that size? What alignment property does it exploit?

12. NodePool uses per-search iteration counters instead of clearing arrays between searches. What problem does this solve, and what is the complexity difference?

13. LIFO ordering is used within secondary buckets. Why LIFO instead of FIFO, and what search behavior does it encourage?

MEMORY & CACHE
--------------
14. Binary heap has O(log n) push/pop with good asymptotic complexity. Why does it lose to bucket structures on cache metrics in practice?

15. How does the two-level bucket structure affect LLC miss rate compared to a binary heap? What access pattern causes this?

16. When does the bucket approach cause MORE cache pressure than a heap, and under what conditions does that flip?

17. Minor page faults vs. major page faults — what is the difference, and which one dominates during a typical search run in this repo?

18. What is IPC (instructions per cycle), and what does a low IPC during ANA*'s rebuild() tell you about the bottleneck?

19. Why would TLB misses be a concern as BlockPool grows, and what hardware mechanism causes them?

IMPLEMENTATION & SYSTEMS
------------------------
20. You use perf_event_open directly instead of calling perf stat. What does this give you that the CLI tool does not?

21. The build uses -O3 -march=native -flto. What does each flag do, and what profiling concern does -fno-omit-frame-pointer address?

22. The codebase uses C++20 in CMakeLists but no C++20 features. If you dropped to C++17, what would change?

23. How does the PriorityCalculator template parameter decouple priority logic from the bucket heap data structure?

24. You have environments for Grid, Sliding Tile, Pancake, and MSA. What property must every environment expose for the algorithm templates to work?

TRADEOFFS & BROADER CONTEXT
----------------------------
25. The paper this extends targets bounded-suboptimal and anytime search specifically. Why are bucket-based structures a better fit for these algorithms than for plain A*?
